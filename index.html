jquery.Gridifier

Perfectly scale and center images in beautiful grids. Works with every type of container (fixed size, max size, percentual size, auto). With a few options for alignment, resizing, and description.

Usage and settings are explained in the demo page, but it's as easy as:

  1. Importing gridifier's CSS and JS.
  2. Calling them on your image containers
  3. Enjoying the results
